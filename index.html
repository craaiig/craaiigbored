How It Works

The tuner autocorrelates the live input and can mute the whole rig while you tune. Overdrive uses asymmetric clipping plus pre/post EQ to lean into a raw 90s grunge voice. Chorus modulates a short delay line with an LFO, delay adds repeats with a capped feedback loop, and the spring reverb generates a metallic synthetic impulse response before the looper tap. The cabinet simulator then shapes the post-loop mix before master level, the oscilloscope, and WAV output recording.
