How It Works

Overdrive uses asymmetric clipping plus pre/post EQ to lean into a raw 90s grunge voice. Delay adds repeats with a capped feedback loop. The cabinet simulator builds a synthetic 4x12 impulse response in JavaScript and sits after the looper tap, before master level and the oscilloscope. Low Latency mode temporarily sheds the cab sim and scope load for faster live monitoring, and the output recorder captures the finished mix for WAV export.
